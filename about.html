About Us

Welcome to Stop Fraud Scammers, your ultimate resource for combating fraudulent activity in the oil and gas industry. Our website serves as a valuable tool for uncovering fake buyers and sellers of oil and gas, as well as petroleum products. We aim to protect legitimate sellers, refiners, and title holders from falling victim to scams by providing a comprehensive directory of known scammers in the industry. We understand the importance of verifying the authenticity of potential business partners, especially when dealing with high-value transactions. That's why we focus on exposing individuals and companies who engage in deceptive practices, particularly those who are only interested in obtaining sellers' Proof of Product (POP) documents without any intention of completing a legitimate transaction.

By utilizing our platform, you can access vital information to help you make informed decisions and avoid becoming a victim of fraud. Together, we can work towards creating a safer and more transparent marketplace for all participants in the oil and gas sector. Join us in the fight against fraud and help us stop scammers in their tracks. Together, we can make a difference.
